About Me

I'm a creative Front-End Designer/Developer with a passion for building memorable and user-friendly websites from scratch.

My journey into Web Development started in high school when I began learning basic HTML and CSS on my own to personalize MySpace layouts. I did not pursue coding, rather went on to complete degrees in Digital Media and Integrated Communications due to my long time passion for print and packaging design. At this time, I also grew professionally as an educator.

I worked with pre-k for six years, but because I wanted to share my love for design, I moved up teach sixth grade technology literacy. During this time, I taught my sixth graders how to code using the programming language Scratch. This is what reignited my love for coding, the need to challenge myself, and led me to transition to a career in Web Development.

I graduated from the Full Stack Coding Bootcamp at Austin where I excelled in building numerous web applications by integrating multiple languages, platforms, and technologies.

Today, I am able to combine my design and coding skills to build user-focused web applications while continuously challenging myself as a designer and developer.
